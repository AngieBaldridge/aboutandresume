Resume

I earned my MFA in painting at the University of the Arts in Philadelphia. I spent eight years teaching art in private art studios and public schools. Most of my teaching career was spent at the highschool level. I have facilitated the design and production of three murals and completed one on my own. I also exhibit my own art work and accept commission projects as time permits.
Recently I have ventured into the world of digital design, completing a UXDI immersive course, and taking graphic design and coding classes.
